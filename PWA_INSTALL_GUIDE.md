# 📱 Add Home Hub to Your Phone's Home Screen

## For iPhone/iPad (Safari)

1. Open Home Hub website in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Edit the name if you want (default: "Home Hub")
5. Tap **"Add"** in the top right
6. 🏠 Home Hub icon will appear on your home screen!

## For Android (Chrome)

1. Open Home Hub website in **Chrome**
2. Tap the **three dots** (⋮) menu in the top right
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
4. Confirm by tapping **"Install"** or **"Add"**
5. 🏠 Home Hub icon will appear on your home screen!

## Benefits

✅ **Full-screen experience** - No browser address bar
✅ **App-like feel** - Opens like a native app
✅ **Quick access** - One tap from home screen
✅ **Always online** - Fetches fresh data every time
✅ **Optimized for mobile** - Touch-friendly buttons and layouts

## Note

- PWA works best on mobile devices
- Always requires internet connection (no offline mode)
- Data syncs instantly across all devices
- Logout applies to all devices using the same account
